Frequently Asked Questions

How does Poka verify the quality of poker games?

We use a comprehensive review system where hosts are verified through ID checks, previous game history, and player reviews. Only hosts with positive ratings and verified identities can list games on our platform.

What stakes and game types will be available?

Poka will support all popular poker variants including Texas Hold'em, Omaha, and tournament formats. Stakes range from micro ($0.25/$0.50) to high stakes ($25/$50+), with detailed filters to find your preferred game type.

Is it legal to use Poka in my area?

Poka connects players to private, home-game poker events which are legal in most jurisdictions. However, we recommend checking your local laws regarding private poker games. We do not facilitate online gambling or unlicensed commercial poker rooms.

How do I ensure my safety when joining a new game?

Every game includes detailed host profiles, player reviews, location verification, and safety guidelines. We recommend starting with lower-stakes games, meeting in public initially, and always informing someone of your plans. Our community guidelines prioritize player safety above all.

When will Poka launch?

We're targeting a beta launch in Q4 2025, starting with major metropolitan areas. Waitlist members will get priority access and exclusive early features. Sign up above to be among the first to know when we go live in your city!

What does it cost to use Poka?

Poka is free for players to discover and join games!
